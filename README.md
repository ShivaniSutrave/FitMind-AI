# FitMind AI 🧠✨

**FitMind AI** is an innovative iOS prototype designed to bridge the gap between mental wellness and physical fitness. Unlike traditional fitness trackers that focus solely on metrics, FitMind AI listens to how you feel to suggest the most effective and sustainable workout for your current emotional state.

## 💡 The Inspiration
Many people struggle with fitness consistency because their apps demand high-intensity workouts when they are feeling burnt out or stressed. I built FitMind AI to provide a "mood-first" approach to exercise, ensuring that staying active always feels supportive, not draining.

## 🚀 Core Features
* **Real-time Mood Analysis:** Input natural language (e.g., "I'm feeling exhausted") to receive an instant, tailored activity suggestion.
* **Dynamic Suggestion Engine:** Maps emotional states—like "Tired," "Happy," or "Alone"—to specific physical activities ranging from restorative stretching to strength training.
* **Mood History Tracking:** Utilizes the **Swift Charts** framework to visualize your emotional journey over time, helping you see the correlation between your feelings and your movement.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
* **Language:** Swift 6.0
* **UI Framework:** SwiftUI
* **Data Viz:** Swift Charts
* **Architecture:** State-driven reactive UI

## 📈 Future Roadmap
* **LLM Integration:** Moving beyond keyword matching to advanced sentiment analysis.
* **Apple HealthKit:** Syncing with Apple Watch biometrics (Heart Rate, HRV, Sleep) to automate mood detection.
* **Predictive Analytics:** Using math to predict peak performance days based on historical mood data.
